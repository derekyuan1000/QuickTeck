Only £99.99(total price), you get 10pcs of your designed boards.


We have tried to make our ordering procedure as simple and flexible as possible.


You can use the  Online quote to obtain an instant price: the price you get is the total price you pay.

Place your order

You have several options when placing an order.
Alternatively you can select from the offer list for deals at even lower prices.

Option1 - Online order

 Use Order now to place your order on line. Just four steps, it's easy, flexible and reliable.

  Step 1: Please be sure that you have entered all your contact information. This will be used to contact you in case of a problem with your order.

  Step 2: Please enter the correct information about your board in the relevant areas on PCB order form. If you are unsure of something, just use the default option, we will sort it out for you. Please note, for some special offers, we might only give you the standard options(e.g. thickness, surface, finish, etc. This will be informed in the order confirmation email.).

  Step 3: Please prepare all your files as a single ZIP/RAR archive and upload it. If there are any queries or issues, we will try to contact you ASAP(normally less than 8 hours after we receive cleared payment).

  Step 4: You will need to use PayPal to checkout in this step. Even you don't have a PayPal account, it doesn't matter. You can still pay with your credit/debit card through PayPal [How to do]. Please note we never see your credit/debit card details.
We also accept bank transfers for the payment.  If you like to pay through this way, please Contact us to get the necessary information.

The order process will be complete after the above four steps. We will send you an confirmation by email once the order is completed.

Option2 - offer list

Alternatively you can select from the offer list for deals at even lower prices.
The deal service is quite easy: just select your deal of choice and upload your design file. Leave the rest to us. We will send you an order confirmation by email.

Option3 - Offline order

For those large volume or any other orders which the online-order is not suitable for, please send us your CAM file and the custom requirements by email. We will come back to you with the request quote in less than 8 hours.

We will perform some basic checks on your updated files in less than 4 hours after receiving the order. If there are any queries or issues, we will contact you immediately. So, please keep an eye on your email in the first 4 hours.

Your fabrication files will be sent to Chinese factory in less than 12 hours. Once our contacts there finish the company's standard checks, the manufacturing process will start and be completed within 48-96 hours of us receiving your order.

A tracking number will be sent to you via email once your boards are being despatched so you can track them online. If you have any special requirement on the shipping, please contact us before the boards are due to be delivered.
