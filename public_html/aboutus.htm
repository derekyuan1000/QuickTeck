We are a family-owned & run company based in Hertfordshire, UK. We specialise in PCB prototype and large volume PCB production. Our fabrication services offer excellent quality and a high level of customisation to suit low budgets.

Before we moved to the UK, we had been managing a profitable business in China for more than 12 years. We own and manage our China-based PCB manufacturing factory and don't use any sub-contractors for manufacturing. This has allowed us to offer our UK customers the following benefits:

►Reliable quality
We comply with the ISO9001:2000 quality management system and UL approvals. All manufacture of PCBs is subject to statistical process control methodology. 

We perform electrical tests for all multilayer PCBs. It is our aim to provide the best quality products. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, we guarantee to replace boards at no charge. You also get a 10% discount on your next order.
